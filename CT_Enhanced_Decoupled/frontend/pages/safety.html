Surveillance & Security

HD CCTV Network

Comprehensive HD camera coverage across all platforms, concourses, entry points, and parking zones. Monitored 24/7 by trained personnel.

Baggage X-Ray Scanning

Advanced X-ray baggage screening systems at all station entry points. All luggage is scanned for prohibited and suspicious items.

RPF Patrolling

Railway Protection Force personnel patrol the station premises round the clock ensuring passenger safety and security.

On-Site Police Station

Government Railway Police (GRP) station located within the terminal premises for immediate law enforcement support.

Emergency Response

Trained staff for rapid emergency response. First aid kits available at key locations throughout the station.

Adequate Lighting

Well-lit platforms, walkways, parking areas, and all public zones to ensure visibility and safety at all hours.

Safety Guidelines

Arrive Early

Passengers are advised to arrive at least 30 minutes before scheduled train departure to allow time for security checks.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you observe any suspicious person, object, or activity, immediately inform RPF staff or call the emergency helpline.

Unattended Luggage

Do not leave your luggage unattended at any time. Report any abandoned or suspicious bags to security personnel immediately.
