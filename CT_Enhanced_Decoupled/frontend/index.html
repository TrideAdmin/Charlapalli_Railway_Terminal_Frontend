What We Offer

Passenger Facilities

Everything you need for a comfortable journey at Charlapalli Terminal

Accessibility

Lifts, escalators, FOBs connecting all platforms. Divyangjan toilets and dedicated assistance.

Waiting Halls

AC & Prepaid AC halls with recliner seating, charging points. ₹40/hr standard, ₹60/hr recliner.

Smart Lockers

Digital lockers in M, L & XL sizes. From ₹60/6hrs. Available at new terminal building.

Food Court

Food plaza on first floor, multipurpose stalls on all platforms and FOBs. RO drinking water everywhere.

Smart & Digital

High-speed free Wi-Fi, LED train info displays, mobile charging stations, multilingual PA system.

Sleeping Pods

Separate ladies and gents pods on first floor of new terminal building.

Hygiene & Comfort

Pay & use and Divyangjan toilets at Platform 01 and new terminal first floor.

Connectivity

TSRTC buses, prepaid autos & taxis, 2/4-wheeler parking with CCTV surveillance.

Safety

HD CCTV across all areas, 24/7 RPF monitoring, advanced baggage X-ray scanners.

Station Navigator

Interactive map with 9 platforms. Point-to-point navigation — find your platform in seconds.
